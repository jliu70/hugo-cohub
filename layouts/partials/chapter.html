Chapter Info

What is included in the Program

  • Net Love partners with Recycleballs.org to recycle used tennis balls.
  • Once an application has been approved, a setup package will be sent to you consisting of a 10 pack of bins, posters, and instructions. These bins come with pre-attached, prepaid UPS shipping labels.
  • Each bin holds exactly 200 balls. A 10 pack is good for 2000 balls.
  • You will receive a tax deduction for every ball shipped to us. Many of our partner facilities claim .30 – .40 per ball.
  • All bins are tracked and you will receive replacement bins (10 pack) automatically after the 5th bin is returned.

What are the benefits of this program

  • For your players - court-side convenience (if you place bins at each court).
  • For your organization - a green “feel good” that players will love.
  • For the environment - A sustainable solution that reduces landfill waste.
  • For your bottom line- a significant potential tax benefit.

How can we get started?

  • Simply fill in the form below.
  • Chapters can start this program on an annual basis with Net Love as a sponsor for the first year. After the first year, if Net Love has funds, Net Love would continue as sponsor. Else it would be best to acquire a new sponsor to fund and extend this program. Net Love and Recycleballs can give you tips to easily find a sponsor.
